Submit a home decor guest post to Fifti Fifti and showcase your decorating expertise.

We feature articles on decorating tips, trends, and inspiration for every room. Whether you're a professional decorator, home stager, or passionate hobbyist, we welcome your guest post submissions.

## Why Guest Post With Us?

- **Decor-Loving Audience**: Readers actively seeking decorating inspiration
- **Quality Backlink**: Relevant do-follow link from an established decor site
- **Build Your Brand**: Position yourself as a decor expert
- **Evergreen Content**: Your article generates ongoing traffic

## What We're Looking For

We're specifically interested in thoughtful, well-researched **home decor guest posts** that offer practical, detailed guidance on decorating and styling homes.

Whether it's helping readers accessorize their spaces, choose the right textiles, arrange art, or refresh rooms on a budget—we want content that's genuinely helpful and inspiring.

## Topic Ideas

- Seasonal decorating ideas
- Budget decorating tips
- Accessorizing and styling spaces
- Wall art and gallery wall arrangements
- Textile and fabric choices
- Room-by-room decor guides

## Submission Requirements

- At least 1,000 words of original, high-quality content
- 1 do-follow link to your site (relevant resources only)
- Properly cited statistics with links to sources
- Non-promotional—no product or service pitches
- Not published elsewhere

## How to Submit

Send your pitch to us with your topic idea and a brief outline. Include links to your previous work. If approved, submit your draft as a Google Doc within two weeks.
