Want to submit an article to Fifti Fifti? We're always looking for quality contributors.

Whether you're an industry expert, professional writer, or passionate enthusiast, we welcome article submissions on home improvement, interior design, decor trends, and lifestyle topics.

## Who We're Looking For

We accept articles from:

- **Industry Professionals**: Interior designers, contractors, real estate agents, home stagers
- **Writers & Bloggers**: Experienced home and lifestyle content creators
- **Enthusiasts**: Passionate DIYers and home improvement hobbyists with valuable insights

## Topics We Cover

- Interior design principles and trends
- Home decor and styling
- Home improvement and renovation
- DIY projects and tutorials
- Real estate and property
- Lifestyle, organization, and wellness
- Sustainable and eco-friendly living

## Article Guidelines

- **Length**: Minimum 800 words, ideally 1,000-2,000
- **Quality**: Well-researched, original, and genuinely helpful
- **Format**: Clear structure with headers, lists where appropriate
- **Links**: One relevant link to your site allowed
- **Images**: Optional but welcome (provide sources/credits)

## Submission Requirements

- Content must be original and not published elsewhere
- No promotional content or product pitches
- Properly cited statistics and external references
- Professional quality writing and grammar
- Relevant to our audience of homeowners and design enthusiasts

## How It Works

1. **Submit Your Pitch**: Email us with your topic idea and brief outline
2. **Wait for Approval**: We review pitches within 3-5 business days
3. **Write Your Article**: Once approved, submit your draft as a Google Doc
4. **Edit & Publish**: We'll review, make any needed edits, and publish

## Contact Us

Ready to submit? Email your pitch to hello@fifti-fifti.net

Include:
- Your proposed topic
- A brief outline (3-5 bullet points)
- Links to your previous writing
- The site you'd like to link to
