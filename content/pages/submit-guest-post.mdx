Ready to submit a guest post to Fifti Fifti? You're in the right place.

We accept high-quality guest posts on home decor, interior design, home improvement, lifestyle, and related topics. Our submission process is simpleâ€”pitch your idea, get approved, and submit your draft within two weeks.

Quality matters to us. We're not looking for thin content stuffed with links. We want genuine expertise that helps our readers create better homes and lives.

## What We Accept

We publish guest posts on topics including:

- **Home Decor**: Decorating tips, styling advice, seasonal ideas
- **Interior Design**: Design principles, trends, room makeovers
- **Home Improvement**: Renovation guides, DIY projects, maintenance tips
- **Lifestyle**: Organization, wellness, work-from-home, sustainable living
- **Real Estate**: Buying, selling, staging, property value

## Content Requirements

- Original, unpublished content only
- Minimum 800 words, thoroughly researched
- Relevant to home, decor, design, or lifestyle topics
- Professional quality writing
- One contextual link per post
- Properly cited statistics and sources

## What We Don't Accept

- Product reviews or promotional content
- Syndicated or previously published articles
- Thin content under 800 words
- Gambling, adult, or pharmaceutical topics
- Poorly written or AI-generated fluff

## Guest Posting Rates

We offer both free and paid guest post options:

- **Free Guest Post**: Exceptionally high-quality content with genuine value
- **Paid Guest Post**: $30 for standard guest posts
- **Link Insertion**: $20 to add a link to existing content

## Submission Process

1. **Pitch Your Idea**: Send us your topic and brief outline
2. **Get Approved**: We'll respond within 3-5 business days
3. **Write Your Draft**: Submit as a Google Doc within 2 weeks
4. **Review & Publish**: We'll edit and publish within 1 week

## How to Submit

Email us at hello@fifti-fifti.net with:

- Your proposed topic and outline
- Links to 2-3 writing samples
- The site you'd like to link to
- Whether you're interested in free or paid placement
