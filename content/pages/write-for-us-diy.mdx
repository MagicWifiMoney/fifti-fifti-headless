Love DIY projects? Fifti Fifti accepts guest posts from DIY enthusiasts, crafters, and home improvement hobbyists.

Our readers enjoy hands-on projects they can tackle themselves—from simple weekend crafts to more ambitious home improvements. If you have step-by-step tutorials, money-saving DIY tips, or creative project ideas, we want to hear from you.

## What We're Looking For

We're specifically interested in thoughtful, well-researched **DIY guest posts** that offer practical, detailed guidance on home projects readers can do themselves.

Whether it's helping readers complete a weekend project, upcycle furniture, save money on home improvements, or learn new crafting skills—we want content that's genuinely helpful, informative, and achievable.

## Topic Ideas We Love

- Weekend DIY projects for beginners
- Budget-friendly home improvement DIYs
- Upcycling and repurposing furniture
- DIY decor ideas for every room
- Tool guides for home DIYers
- Step-by-step renovation tutorials
- Money-saving home repair tips

## Guest Post Guidelines

- At least 1,000 words of high quality and unique content
- 1 do-follow link allowed to your site (resources, blogs, educational content)
- All external statistics must be properly cited with links to original sources
- Non-promotional content only—no product or service mentions
- No syndicated, copied, or plagiarized content
- Original topics we haven't already covered

## How to Submit

Fill out our contact form with your pitch idea and a brief outline. If accepted, we'll request your draft within two weeks, submitted as a Google Doc.
