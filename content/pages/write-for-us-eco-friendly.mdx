Passionate about sustainable living? Fifti Fifti welcomes eco-friendly guest posts from environmental advocates, green living experts, and sustainability professionals.

We're committed to helping our readers make environmentally conscious choices in their homes and lives. If you have practical tips for reducing environmental impact while maintaining a beautiful living space, we'd love to feature your content.

## What We're Looking For

We're specifically interested in thoughtful, well-researched **eco-friendly guest posts** that offer practical, detailed guidance on sustainable home living.

Whether it's helping readers choose eco-friendly materials, reduce household waste, make energy-efficient improvements, or adopt greener habits—we want content that's genuinely helpful, informative, and actionable.

## Topic Ideas We Love

- Eco-friendly home materials and alternatives
- Energy-efficient home improvements
- Sustainable decor and furnishing options
- Reducing household waste and plastic
- Green cleaning products and methods
- Sustainable gardening practices
- Upcycling and repurposing projects

## Guest Post Guidelines

- At least 1,000 words of high quality and unique content
- 1 do-follow link allowed to your site (resources, blogs, educational content)
- All external statistics must be properly cited with links to original sources
- Non-promotional content only—no product or service mentions
- No syndicated, copied, or plagiarized content
- Original topics we haven't already covered

## How to Submit

Fill out our contact form with your pitch idea and a brief outline. If accepted, we'll request your draft within two weeks, submitted as a Google Doc.
