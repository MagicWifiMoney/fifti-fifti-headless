Have expertise in furniture design, selection, or restoration? Fifti Fifti accepts guest posts from furniture enthusiasts, designers, and industry professionals.

Our readers love learning about furniture trends, buying guides, care tips, and how to choose pieces that transform their spaces. Whether you're a furniture designer, antique collector, or retail expert, we want to hear your insights.

## What We're Looking For

We're specifically interested in thoughtful, well-researched **furniture guest posts** that offer practical, detailed guidance on selecting, caring for, and arranging furniture.

Whether it's helping readers choose the right sofa for their space, restore vintage pieces, optimize small apartments, or mix design styles—we want content that's genuinely helpful, informative, and engaging.

## Topic Ideas We Love

- Furniture buying guides for specific rooms
- How to mix furniture styles effectively
- Sustainable and eco-friendly furniture options
- Furniture care and maintenance tips
- Vintage vs. modern furniture choices
- Space-saving furniture for small homes
- Quality indicators when furniture shopping

## Guest Post Guidelines

- At least 1,000 words of high quality and unique content
- 1 do-follow link allowed to your site (resources, blogs, educational content)
- All external statistics must be properly cited with links to original sources
- Non-promotional content only—no product or service mentions
- No syndicated, copied, or plagiarized content
- Original topics we haven't already covered

## How to Submit

Fill out our contact form with your pitch idea and a brief outline. If accepted, we'll request your draft within two weeks, submitted as a Google Doc.
