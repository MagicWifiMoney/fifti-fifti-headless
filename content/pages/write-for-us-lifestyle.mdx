Looking to contribute to a lifestyle blog? Fifti Fifti accepts high-quality lifestyle guest posts from writers who have genuine insights to share.

We cover a broad range of lifestyle topics including home living, personal wellness, sustainable choices, productivity, and creating spaces that enhance daily life. If you have expertise in making life better—whether through design, habits, or mindful living—we'd love to hear from you.

## What We're Looking For

We're specifically interested in thoughtful, well-researched **lifestyle guest posts** that offer practical, detailed guidance on everyday living, home life, personal development, and modern lifestyle topics.

Whether it's helping readers organize their daily routines, create meaningful home spaces, balance work and life, or adopt healthier habits—we want content that's genuinely helpful, informative, and engaging.

## Topic Ideas We Love

- Morning routines that boost productivity
- Creating a balanced work-from-home lifestyle
- Sustainable living tips for everyday life
- Wellness practices for busy professionals
- Minimalism and intentional living
- Home organization strategies that stick
- Work-life balance in the modern age

## Guest Post Guidelines

- At least 1,000 words of high quality and unique content
- 1 do-follow link allowed to your site (resources, blogs, educational content)
- All external statistics must be properly cited with links to original sources
- Non-promotional content only—no product or service mentions
- No syndicated, copied, or plagiarized content
- Original topics we haven't already covered

## How to Submit

Fill out our contact form with your pitch idea and a brief outline. If accepted, we'll request your draft within two weeks, submitted as a Google Doc.
