Interested in contributing real estate content? Fifti Fifti welcomes guest posts from real estate professionals, investors, and industry experts.

Our readers are homeowners and aspiring buyers interested in property insights, market trends, and making smart real estate decisions. We're particularly interested in content that bridges real estate with home design, renovation ROI, and creating valuable living spaces.

## What We're Looking For

We're specifically interested in thoughtful, well-researched **real estate guest posts** that offer practical, detailed guidance on buying, selling, staging, and investing in property.

Whether it's helping readers navigate the home buying process, stage their home for a faster sale, understand market trends, or evaluate investment opportunities—we want content that's genuinely helpful, informative, and engaging.

## Topic Ideas We Love

- Home improvements that increase property value
- First-time homebuyer tips and mistakes to avoid
- Real estate market trends and analysis
- Staging tips for selling your home faster
- Investment property considerations
- When to renovate vs. when to move
- Understanding property values in your area

## Guest Post Guidelines

- At least 1,000 words of high quality and unique content
- 1 do-follow link allowed to your site (resources, blogs, educational content)
- All external statistics must be properly cited with links to original sources
- Non-promotional content only—no product or service mentions
- No syndicated, copied, or plagiarized content
- Original topics we haven't already covered

## How to Submit

Fill out our contact form with your pitch idea and a brief outline. If accepted, we'll request your draft within two weeks, submitted as a Google Doc.
