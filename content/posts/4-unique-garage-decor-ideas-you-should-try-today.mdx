If you are lucky enough to have a garage, it is wise to keep it in optimal shape. When it comes to renovating a home, a garage is often overlooked by homeowners. Giving your garage a makeover will not only help you protect your car but also allow you to store items, such as sports equipment, gardening equipment, etc.

And of course, you want to make it look attractive, too. So, are you looking to decorate your garage but don’t know where to start? Fear not! Here we have curated a list of top unique garage decor ideas you should try to give your garage an instant makeover. So, let’s get started:

## Change Your Garage Flooring

Your garage tends to attract moisture. Therefore, you must invest in flooring that makes your garage waterproof. According to experts, investing in a high-quality [garage floor refinishing](https://thefloorboys.com/){data-affiliate="431"} can be a better option. The epoxy coating seals the cement, preventing moisture from seeping into the material.

Apart from protecting your garage from moisture, it can also make your garage look attractive and well-organized. The beauty of epoxy floors is that you can customize them as per your preferences. For example, you can choose the design, patterns, and colors that suit your garage type.

Compared with other types of floor, epoxy tends to be more durable. It is constructed differently from other flooring options.

## Add Garage Storage

One of the best ways to decorate and organize your garage is to [add storage](https://www.familyhandyman.com/list/brilliant-ways-to-organize-your-garage/){data-affiliate="432"} spaces. One of the obvious benefits of adding storage is that it keeps things organized. Also, it makes it easier for you to access the items whenever you need them.

Moreover, adding storage space can prevent clutter from accumulating. However, when it comes to adding storage to garage space, there are tons of options to choose from. From adding vertical spaces to utilizing the wall space, the opportunities are endless!

## Don’t Forget Linens

Window curtains have the potential to instantly uplift the appearance of any space, including your garage. While turning your garage into a liveable space is great, a few small upgrades will create cohesiveness with the rest of your home.

## **R**eplace Your Garage Door

When decorating a garage, you shouldn’t overlook the exteriors. Giving your exteriors a makeover is as important as the interiors. After all, this is the first thing that your guests will notice.

So, the best way to give your exterior an uplift is by replacing your old garage door. When was the last time you replaced your doors? If you don’t remember, it’s time to replace your traditional doors with new ones.

According to experts, installing [insulated garage doors](https://www.hgtv.com/how-to/home-improvement/how-to-insulate-a-garage-door){data-affiliate="433"} can be your best bet. They are durable and can give your garage an instant uplift. Also, it can keep your garage cooler in summers and warmer in winters. However, [replacing your garage doors](https://gatewaydoorstl.com/garage-door-installation/){data-affiliate="434"} is not a DIY thing. It is wise to call professionals to get the job done.

## Summing Up

Well, the list doesn’t end up here!

When it comes to decorating a garage, the opportunities are endless! Getting a fresh coat of paint, replacing window treatments, and hanging wall art are some other ideas that can enhance the curb appeal.
