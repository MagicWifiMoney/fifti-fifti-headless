When it comes to creating realtor's postcards, there are a few mistakes that you will want to avoid. By avoiding these mistakes, you can design a postcard that is effective and will help generate leads for your business. 

The following are common mistakes that occur when creating [postcards for real estate](https://www.wisepelican.com/truth-about-real-estate-postcards/){data-affiliate="481"}.

## 1. Not Using a Professional Design

Not using a professional design is one of the biggest mistakes. The design should be eye-catching and reflect your brand. If you do not have a professional designer, there are many templates available online that you can use. Here are some key characteristics of a professional design.

**Call-To-Action: **Your call to action should be clear and concise. It should tell the reader what you want them to do, such as "call for a free consultation" or "visit our website." 

**Easy to Read and Understand: **You want to ensure that it is easy to read and understand. Keep the text to a minimum so that it doesn't make it difficult for the reader to digest all the information. 

## 2. Not Personalizing the Postcard for Each Recipient

When creating them, it is important to personalize them for each recipient. This means that you should take the time to research each individual and include information that is relevant to them. For example, if you are targeting buyers, you may want to include information about recent home sales in their area. If you are targeting sellers, you may want to include information about your experience and success in selling homes. 

Personalizing it will help you stand out from the competition and show your recipients that you are knowledgeable and resourceful.

## 3. **Failing to Include Key Information**

When creating a postcard for real estate, it is essential to include critical information. This means you should include information about your company, services, and contact information. It would help if you also highlighted your recent [achievements](https://blog.hubspot.com/sales/real-estate-postcard-ideas){data-affiliate="482"} and successes.

Not including crucial information on it will make it difficult for the reader to understand what you are trying to promote, making it difficult for them to contact you if they have any questions.

Key information that should be included:

-Your company name and logo

-Your contact information (phone number, email address, website)

-A brief description of your services

-Your recent achievements and

## **4. Sending Out Postcards at the Wrong Time of Year**

Sending out postcards at the wrong time of year can be a big and costly mistake. You may pay more for the postage if you send them out during the off-season.

If you are targeting buyers, you may want to send them out in the spring or summer. If you are targeting sellers, you may want to send it out in the fall or winter.

Timing is the most crucial factor when it comes to real estate postcards. You want to ensure that you are sending them out when people are most likely to be interested in buying or selling a home.

## 5. **Not Testing Different Versions Before Sending It Out**

Testing different versions of your postcard before sending it out is a good idea. It will help you determine which version is the most effective. In addition, you may want to test different designs, fonts, or colors. You may also want to test different headlines and call to action.

By following these tips, you can create an effective real estate postcard that will help generate leads for your business. And avoiding these common mistakes will give you a head start in creating a successful marketing campaign.
