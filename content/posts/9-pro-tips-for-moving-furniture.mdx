## **9 Ways To Relocate Big Furniture**

### **1. Measure First**

Before you lift and move the furniture, you need to make sure it can go through the door. Also, if you are moving it downstairs, you need to consider that as well. Your sofa might fit the elevator but it may not go through your door. 

Therefore, you need to measure the doorways and the hallways. This will help you know whether the large pieces of furniture will fit your front door. Keep in mind that you might have the same problems when you get to the new home. 

### **2. Disassemble the Furniture**

If your big furniture cannot pass your apartment door, the best option is to disassemble it. Moreover, bulky furniture such as lounges and beds are easier to relocate when you disassemble them. Therefore, if you can manage to disassemble the furniture, it is a good idea to do that. 

Although it might create an additional job for your, you will be able to move smaller and lighter pieces of furniture, and you will protect the item from any damages. Also, wrap and protect any piece of furniture before loading it to the moving truck. 

### **3. Consider Sliding**

If it is possible, consider sliding the furniture instead of lifting them. However, you need to ensure the floorboards and the tiles are well protected. You can use cardboard or towels to protect the floors. Hence, do not strain yourself by pushing big pieces of furniture. 

### **4. Ensure You Protect the Furniture**

You need to ensure your furniture pieces are safe when moving them from one area to another. Therefore, make sure you use high-quality packing materials. Also, when loading the furniture to the moving truck, consider other items being packed. 

Keep in mind that furniture pieces are bulky and large and can cause damages to other things in the moving truck. So, ensure everything is well packed and properly secured in the truck.
