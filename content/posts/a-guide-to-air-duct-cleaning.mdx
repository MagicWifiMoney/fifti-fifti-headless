Every system needs to be cleaned and repaired from time to time. And your HVAC system is no exception. If you choose not to clean it, you run the risk of many repercussions. This includes a wide range of consequences from a simple system failure to serious illness for everyone living in an HVAC-contaminated home. How to choose an air [duct cleaning company](https://amazonairpro.com/){data-affiliate="929"}? Why should you clean air ducts regularly? Let's consider all this in more detail.

## Why should you clean your ducts?

Air duct cleaning is important. If you ignore this issue, you may experience:

- Failure of the entire HVAC system. The breakdown can be both repairable and unrepairable, up to the need to replace the entire system.
- HVAC can accumulate a lot of dust, mold, and other debris. Needless to say, such a neighboring is unpleasant and even dangerous.
- If you are allergic to dust or mold, then you run the risk of experiencing discomfort even after a complete cleaning of the entire house.
- You can get a wide range of illnesses by exposing yourself to polluted air for too long: ranging from simple allergies (even if you didn't have one ever before) to obstructive pulmonary disease and lung cancer.

You have already understood that cleaning this system is important. Is it possible to carry out this procedure yourself?

## Cleaning ducts yourself or using someone's help?

Briefly, cleaning such a system without the necessary knowledge and equipment is almost impossible. HVAC is a complex system. Many of its parts may be inaccessible for visual inspection without the necessary equipment and knowledge of where to look. It is also worth saying that when you try to do everything yourself, you risk breaking it. That is why the simplest and most logical solution is to seek professional help. But what to look for when choosing a duct cleaning services provider?

## How to choose an air duct cleaning company

If there is only one such company in your city, you can skip this paragraph. If there are several, then here are some tips to help you decide:

- Reputation is extremely important. What do people say about this company? What reviews do you find? But don't pay attention to a single bad review. Only a systematically large number of negative reviews can speak of a bad reputation.
- Check if the company has the necessary equipment. Since cleaning air ducts without industrial-grade equipment may not be possible.
- What additional services can be provided to you? Since air duct cleaning may not be all you need. Perhaps the air filters need to be replaced as well? Or does something need to be fixed? Maybe there are insects in your system and they need to be removed from there.
- What guarantees are given to you in case something goes wrong? This happens extremely rarely, but still, those who have to fix things can, on the contrary, break them. Will you be compensated for this damage?

The main thing not to forget – the cleaning procedure must be carried out regularly. How often should this be done? This question will be answered by professionals who should clean your system. On average, this is once every few months or once every half a year, or even once a year. The older the system, the more often you need to clean it. Also, remember to change your air filters regularly. On average, this should be done once every few months or more often, depending on the filter type being used.
