## **Different Services for Heating and Cooling **

- **HVAC Installation- **involves an assessment of your home, any current system(s) you may have, ductwork, etc. Determining the right-sized equipment needed for the installation involves counseling and consideration into temperature control, efficiency, sound, etc. And of course, the actual installation of the HVAC equipment.** **

- **HVAC Repair and Maintenance- **involves any repair or maintenance on the HVAC equipment. This can involve repairing or even replacing certain parts, ensuring that everything is working properly, safely, and efficiently, and more. Some companies will require a diagnostic fee for this service, to help cover the costs of travel and such. A separate quote will then be provided for any repairs or maintenance discovered on-site. 

### Some other aspects of the maintenance service can include:

- The measurement and testing of key components which can include voltage amounts, temperature amounts, pressure amounts, and more help ensure that the components are in working order and within the manufacturer's specifications. 

- Checking airflow and wiring, refrigerant cooling levels and thermostat adjustment, and basically overall system performance.
