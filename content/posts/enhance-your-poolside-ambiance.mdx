Summer weather means more time in the sun and hanging out in the backyard pool for many people. Cookouts and swim parties with family and friends are in full swing, with summer heat fueling the festivities. Watching the billowy, white clouds drift by in the ultra-blue sky or stargazing into the inky night becomes a favorite activity when comfortable furnishings adorn the pool area. 

There is a plethora of choices when shopping for [outdoor furniture](https://fifti-fifti.net/patio/5-tips-for-buying-outdoor-furniture/){data-affiliate="1840"}, from frugal plastic stackable chairs to plush wicker models made for outdoor patios. For the best outdoor furnishings, consider shopping for commercial pool furniture online or in person at a furniture supply house. 

## Outdoor Dining

Good-quality resin stacking chairs are made to withstand the sun's heat easily. Coupled with a quality outdoor dining table, [Commercial Pool Furniture](https://outdoorsiness.com/product-category/commercial-pool-furniture/){data-affiliate="1841"} makes sense for prolonged use in this application. For added comfort, add standard seat cushions to your stacking chairs that can be removed in the case of inclement weather. If adding an umbrella to your dining table, purchase a table with the hole predrilled for easy installation. Consider adding a large outdoor storage container to the mix to store away cushions and other soft materials when not in use. 

## Umbrellas

An outdoor umbrella for your seating arrangement offers a respite from the glaring sun or protection from light rain in the case of showers. Quality umbrellas from a commercial pool furniture outlet are engineered to withstand the elements with durable corrosion-resistant materials and quality fabrics. These weather-resistant models are reinforced for long-term durability. Look for a weighted umbrella stand for stability. 

## Pool Chairs

The plastic and fabrics from standard pool chairs can quickly become degraded with sunscreen and other protective body lotions and oils. Additionally,  the sun’s ultraviolet rays can lead to material breakdowns in inexpensive poolside [furnishings](https://www.bhg.com/home-improvement/patio/designs/8-tips-patio-furniture/){data-affiliate="1842"}. Buying quality pool furniture can save you money in the long run. It has UV-resistant materials manufactured from high-quality marine-grade materials that are easily washable to maintain the integrity of the surface.

## Side Tables

For drinks and snacks poolside, purchase a few side tables to place by your seating arrangement. Having someplace to rest your libation provides comfortable seating for guests to relax easily. Consider purchasing a few sizes of [tables](https://www.bobvila.com/articles/types-of-tables/){data-affiliate="1843"}, including varieties suited to chair-height and bar-height arrangements. You can find tables in various materials and styles to match most decor with aluminum, glass, and acrylic model table tops with corrosion-resistant frames and safety glass in pool furniture.  

## Quality Furnishings That Last

Consider purchasing your next dining set and poolside chairs from a commercial pool furniture manufacturer for poolside furnishings made to go the distance. Buying quality tables and chairs may be more expensive, but you will save money by not replacing your poolside equipment in only a few short seasons. You can easily find stylish supplies to coordinate with your pool area in major retailers online or in person. Upgrade to commercial-grade furniture today to experience quality furnishings that last.
