Exterior doors are the first thing that visitors see when they enter your home. It is essential to choose the right door for your home. Exterior doors are either made of wood, steel, glass, or fiberglass. A door can transform the look of your home and give it the right ambiance. There are a variety of doors available in the market. Depending on your home’s design, you need to choose the right type of exterior doors for your home.

Entry doors should also be able to meet your needs in terms of security. Front doors are the most visible part of your home and should have an impact on the overall aesthetic appeal of your home. 

# Solid Wood Doors

Solid wood doors are most commonly used for exterior doors. The wooden doors are made of all types of woods such as oak, pine, mahogany, etc. They are not only sturdy but also beautiful to look at. The wood exterior doors come in different shapes such as square, round, oval, etc. They are available in different sizes and styles depending on the kind of door that you choose. You can also choose the color of the door from the wide range of colors available in the market. The wood doors are highly reliable and provide an elegant look to your home.

## Steel Door

Steel doors have been used for decades to provide an attractive look to homes. They are also available in various sizes and shapes. The steel doors come with a variety of finishes such as galvanized, powder coated, etc. These steel doors have a smooth surface which gives a shiny look to your home.

French doors are an excellent option for those who want to add beauty and elegance to their homes. They come in different sizes and shapes depending on the kind of door that you choose.
