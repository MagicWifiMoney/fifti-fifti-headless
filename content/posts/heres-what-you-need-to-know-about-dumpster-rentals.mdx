## **Three Dumpster Options **

Dumpster rental sizing ranges from 15 yards- 40 yards. The three standard sizes you can rent without additional accommodations in weight are 15 yards, 30 yards, and 40 yards. Each size option of dumpster rental Las Vegas is preset to accommodate different types and sizes of cleanup [projects](https://www.theguardian.com/lifeandstyle/shortcuts/2014/jan/29/skipping-food-waste-supermarkets-dumpster-diving){data-affiliate="1199"}. 

## **Projects To Size: dumpster rental Las Vegas**

Here is a breakdown of general project correlations to standard sizes of dumpster rental Las Vegas: 

**15-yard dumpster (roll-off): **

- This dumpster is efficient for smaller cleanup tasks like removing furniture pieces or small room cleanouts.

- It holds approximately seven pickup truckloads worth of materials.

**30-yard dumpster (roll-off):**

- This dumpster is efficient for slightly larger cleanup projects like scraps left over from kitchen remodels, room cleanouts of medium-sized rooms, moving junk, etc. 

- It holds approximately 12 pickup truckloads worth of materials.

**40-yard dumpster (roll-off):**

- This dumpster is for oversized cleanups, including large construction site jobs, estate cleanouts, and the removal of large debris.

- It holds approximately 16 pickup truckloads of material.
