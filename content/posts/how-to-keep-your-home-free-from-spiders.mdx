The most dangerous and terrifying spiders in Australia worry many people. There are an estimated 49,000 species worldwide, with 4 thousand living in Australia. While only a small number of spider species are dangerous to humans, they can still be a nuisance.

In some cases, they can enter your home through open windows or doors or even through minor cracks and crevices. It can be quite a challenge to get rid of them once they're inside. However, with the help of [spider pest control](https://handymate.com.au/vic/spider-control-melbourne){data-affiliate="379"} services, you can keep your home free of these creepy crawlers. 

The following are a few tips on how to keep them out:

## 1. Seal Up Any Cracks or Crevices:

They can quickly enter through the smallest of cracks and crevices. Inspect the exterior of your home and seal up any cracks or gaps you find. Ensure to check around doors and windows and any pipes or cables that enter your home.

## 2. Keep Your Home Clean:

They love to live in dirty, cluttered environments. Keep your place clean and free of clutter to reduce their chances of taking up residence. [Organize your kitchen](https://fifti-fifti.net/kitchen/how-to-organize-a-small-kitchen-with-no-pantry/){data-affiliate="380"} and living spaces to make them easy to clean and maintain your home.

## **3. Remove Spider Webs:**

Regularly remove webs from your home to make it less appealing to them. Check high up on ceilings, corners, windows, and doors.

## **4. Use a Spider Repellent:**

There are several repellents available on the market. Pick one that is safe for use around pets and children.

- **Peppermint Oil:** To keep pesky bugs out, mix 20 drops of peppermint essential oil (not extract) into a 32-ounce spray bottle of water. Spray the mixture in corners near entrances and floorboards around the whole house. You can also shake some directly onto cotton balls to stuff them into crevices where they are suspected to be present.
- **Cinnamon:** Combine one teaspoon cinnamon powder and one cup of water. Spray the mixture in areas where they are commonly found. You can also spread the cinnamon powder around the outside of your home. 
- **Citrus:** Spritz areas where they like to hide with equal parts water and lemon, orange, or grapefruit peel. You can also use the essential oils from these fruits. 
- **Vinegar:** Mix a solution of half vinegar and half water in a spray bottle. Spray spider-infested areas liberally. The vinegar smell will dissipate quickly.

## 5. Use a Vacuum:

Vacuuming is an effective way to keep them away. Be sure to vacuum all webs and any species you see. It's crucial to do this regularly, especially if you have a spider problem.

## **6. Use a Wasp Spray:**

You can also use a Wasp spray to take them out. Just aim it directly at the spider and any webs you see.

## **Which Spiders Are Harmful?**

While most are harmless, a few species can be dangerous to humans. These include the funnel-web spider, the redback spider, and the white-tailed spider.

The funnel-web spider is the most dangerous in the world. It is native to Australia and can be found in Sydney and the surrounding areas. These spiders are giant and aggressive, and their venom is highly toxic.** **
