## How does the Ninja Creami work?

The Ninja Creami redefines the at-home dessert experience by transforming everyday ingredients into gourmet frozen treats. At its core, the device employs an innovative process, blending and freezing components in a manner that's both unique and efficient. Let's dive into the mechanics of the Ninja Creami, to better understand how it operates.

### The process

- **Prep the base and freeze:** The Ninja Creami requires you to pre-freeze your base mixture in its pint containers for at least 24 hours. This step is crucial as it prepares the mixture for the Creami’s unique blending process.

- **Creamify:** Once frozen, the pint is then inserted into the Ninja Creami. With the press of a button, the Creami's blade system spins and shaves the frozen block, incorporating air and breaking down ice crystals. This process, referred to as &#8216;Creamify', transforms the contents into a smooth, creamy texture. Unlike traditional ice cream makers, that require constant churning during the freezing process, the Ninja Creami focuses on texture refinement post-freeze.

- **Customize your dessert:**  After the initial creamification, you can choose to mix in additional ngredients such as chocolate chips or fruit for added texture and flavor. The device offers multiple settings for different dessert types, including ice cream, gelato, milkshakes, and even sorbet, catering to a wide range of dietary preferences and tastes.
