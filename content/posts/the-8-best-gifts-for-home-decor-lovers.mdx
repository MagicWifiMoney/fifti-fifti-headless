We all know that one person who is crazy about interior design. They know how to blend their colors perfectly and drape their walls with awe-inspiring art. The good thing about home deco lovers is that you do not have to break a leg trying to find a gift they would love. 

Here are the best gifts for home décor lovers that are guaranteed to make them swoon.

## Jewelry Tray

Your lady friend will love a bedazzled jewelry tray that will help her keep all her earrings in one place and double up as a stunning home décor piece. 

There are a variety of designs and colors online to choose from. Pick a jewelry tray that blends with their drapes and furniture's overall theme and color.

## Woven Basket

A woven basket is an excellent pick for your stylish friends who would appreciate a bit more storage room for their knits or scarfs. 

They could also use these woven baskets to display in the hallways or carry groceries. Your pal will enjoy being gifted with home décor that is not only gorgeous but also practical.

## Oil Diffusers

Essential oil diffusers are a great alternative to scented candles and will leave any room smelling magnificent. A wide range of [oil diffusers](https://www.forbes.com/sites/forbes-personal-shopper/2021/08/23/best-essential-oil-diffusers/?sh=437fc9bf4c24){data-affiliate="338"} are elegant, powerful, and pocket-friendly to gift your deco-loving pals. Go a step further and choose a diffuser that lights up in several different colors that are not distractingly bright.

## Hands Catchall

A hand's catchall can be cast iron, wood, or ceramic and symbolize helping hands. Gift your pal a hand catchall to show them you will always be there when they need you. 

They could use this decorative piece to store small jewelry, keys, or buttons.

## Personalized Blanket

Nothing says "I love you" more than [personalized blankets with names](https://famiprints.com/collections/fleece-blanket){data-affiliate="339"} or photos of your precious moments together. Whenever they snuggle into this blanket on a chilly night, they will never forget that you got their back in the good and bad times. A personalized blanket is an excellent and sentimental gift that will add to their home décor collection.

## Tic-tac-toe Set

Tic-tac-toe is one of the many fun [board games](https://www.huffingtonpost.co.uk/entry/family-board-games-remind-you-all-how-fun-it-is-to-play-together_uk_59b82e68e4b086432b021082){data-affiliate="340"} to play with friends and family. Why not get your friend a tic-tac-toe set that can double up as their coffee table display? It is too beautiful to be stashed away in a cabinet, whether made of wood or ceramic.

## Flower Vase

If your loved one has a green thumb, it would be a great idea to get them an indoor decorative flower vase. Determine whether your pal loves a more modern or rustic style, and then go hunting for the perfect vase for their home. A great alternative to flower vases would be a unique hanging planter.

## Wreath Kit

For your pals that love to get crafty and are willing to learn a new skill, look no further than a wreath kit. It comes with a copper wreath form, floral tape, and clippers to help your pal develop a customized deco for any time of the year.

Which of these suggestions did you feel resonated with you? We hope that this article has given you an idea of what you can get your loved one who is so crazy about home décor.
