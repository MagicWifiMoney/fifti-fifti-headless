## **Kitchen Remodeling Ideas**

Here are some of the unique kitchen remodeling ideas which can beautify this corner of your house perfectly.

### **1. Experiment With Wood**

Although modern shelves are in style today, you can experiment with some vintage or retro-style kitchens. Wooden cabinets are a staple for this kind of kitchen design. The best part is selecting the perfect wooden design for your kitchen.

For example, if you are more of a fan of rustic design, then go for a cherry red cabinet. On the other hand, if you like the clean and royal design, then go for the French cabinet.

If you are looking for a total vintage design for your kitchen, you can even ask your renovators to build a personalized shelf for your modern appliances like the chimney. You can even play with colors, ditch the normal color of wood, and go for pastel wood statement shelves.  

### **2. A Kitchen Island**

A kitchen island brings such a homely vibe to your kitchen. Like it is not just a kitchen but a space to hang out at the end of the day, have a cup of a warm beverage, or indulge in some therapeutic vegetable cutting sessions.

Since you cannot place decorative items all over the kitchen because of the stove and other edible items lying all around, the kitchen island comes to the rescue. It is like an extension to your kitchen, and you can also use it for decorative purposes.

You can even build shelves all around, underneath the island and acquire extra space for your pantry. 

### **3. Tiled Walls**

This is one of the unique ideas which most kitchen lovers are embracing. Since normal walls are not very confident because of the oil accumulation, tiles are much easier to clean.

Yes, it will take some time to install the entire tiled presentation around the perimeters of your kitchen, but with the right renovating company, you will be able to get it through without much hassle.

Plus, there are an endless series of designs you can choose from, which can even add to the theme of the kitchen. 

### **4. Unique Lighting**

This is not a very explored venture, but mood lighting in the kitchen can make it more than just a space you cook in. This mood lighting can even grow one's enthusiasm to be in the space longer and cook delicious meals.

You can have ceiling hanging lights or even lamps on the kitchen island tops. Anything which can make the space even cozier and more comfortable for you to cook in. 

### **5. Small Kitchen Table Or Countertops**

Small tables and countertops with chairs are common ways to add the comfort of family and home to your kitchen.

You can have a small wooden table for your kitchen. Just a seat of two or more. It could be your space for morning coffee and light breakfast while something is already brewing on the stove.

Plus, one you are not using this, this could be a place for work. Gives you some extra space to work when you are working from home.
