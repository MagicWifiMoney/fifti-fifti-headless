## 2. Have An Estate Sale

You may consider having an estate sale for your old furniture and appliances. You can sign up a board and place it in front of the house and the selected table you're selling.

## 5. Donate Items To Charitable Organizations

It happens to be one of the best ways to get rid of old furniture and appliances. Donating stuff to the charitable institution is a good gesture to the community in that unfortunate people benefit from it.

## 7. Recycle Centres

For recycling items like those made of metal, you can take them to these centers for recycling. They remove metal and wood from the structure and hence can restructure the item at a small fee.

It is the simplest way to restructure your item hence expanding its lifespan.
