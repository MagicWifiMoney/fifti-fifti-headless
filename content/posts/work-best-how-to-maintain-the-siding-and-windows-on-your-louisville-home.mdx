Cleaning your siding and windows is a very important part of maintaining your siding and windows. Without regular cleaning, mold and mildew will grow on the siding and window frames and cause them to rot. This can lead to water damage and in turn, further damage your home.

Regular cleaning will also help to prevent any stains from building up on the siding and windows. If you notice any stains, then it's time for a cleaning. Scaling and sealing your siding is one of the most important maintenance tasks that you have when it comes to maintaining your siding and windows in your home. Scaling and sealing your siding will help to protect it from outdoor elements such as rain, sun, wind, [wintry weather like snow and ice](https://fifti-fifti.net/patio/6-winter-patio-ideas-to-make-your-patio-cozy-in-the-winter/){data-affiliate="680"}, insects, and animals.
